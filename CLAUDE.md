# Weekly Sport Reading — selection notes

This repo is updated by a scheduled weekly task that appends a row to
`data/weekly_reading.csv` (see the trigger prompt for full instructions).

## Analytics item (Item 2) preference

Prefer genuine analytics projects and writeups over sports news that merely
cites analytics figures. Good fits: technical blog posts with methodology,
open-source tools/libraries, dataset or GitHub releases, conference talks or
slides, newsletter issues with analytical depth, public model or methodology
writeups.

Avoid single-game recaps or match reports that just embed a stat like
expected goals, even from reputable analytics outlets (e.g. Opta Analyst game
recaps). Those count as sports news with an analytics tilt, not an analytics
project, and should not be picked even if a project-style item from the last
7 days is harder to find.
